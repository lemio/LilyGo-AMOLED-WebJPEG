Tips:

  • Make sure your ESP32 is connected to WiFi
  • Reachable at esp-webjpeg.local / esp-webh264.local unless you set a different mDNS hostname when flashing
  • Screen share works in Chrome, Edge, and Firefox (WebH264 needs Chrome or Edge for WebCodecs)
  • Grant camera/screen permissions when prompted
  • Display size auto-detects from the board - override the dropdown manually if it's wrong or unreachable
  • Lower frame rate or quality if stream is laggy
  • The on-device decoder tops out around 3-5fps - open the browser console (F12) for detailed per-frame logs

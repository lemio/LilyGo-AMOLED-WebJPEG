Screen → LilyGo T4-S3 AMOLED

Captures your screen, encodes it as H.264 in the browser (WebCodecs), and streams it over a WebSocket to the ESP32, which decodes it and pushes it to the display.

Run this page from a secure context (e.g. python3 -m http.server on your machine and open http://localhost:8000/h264_stream.html) — Chrome only allows screen capture from https:// or http://localhost, not from a plain http:// LAN address or a file:// URL.

Open the browser console (F12) for detailed per-frame logs.
